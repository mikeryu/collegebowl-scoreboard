{\huge\bf Toss-Up Question}

{\Large What is the area of the circle given by the equation $x^2-4x+y^2+2y-20=0$?}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $25\pi$.}

\vspace{2in}

{\huge\bf Follow-Up Question}

{\Large Find the product of the length of the major and minor axes of the ellipse given by the equation $9x^2-18x+4y^2+8y=23$.}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large  $24$}

\clearpage

%-------------------Question/Follow-up 2-------------------------


{\huge\bf Toss-Up Question}

{\Large Recall that a parabola consists of all points equidistant from a point, called the focus, and a line, called the directrix. What is the equation of the directrix of the parabola $y=x^2$?}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $y=-\frac{1}{4}$.}

\vspace{2in}

{\huge\bf Follow-Up Question}

{\Large A searchlight is made in the shape of a parabola with the light source placed at the focus. If the light source is $2$ feet from the vertex and the depth of the searchlight is $4$ feet, what should the width of the opening be?}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $8\sqrt{2}$.}

\clearpage

\vspace{0.75in}




%-------------------Question/Follow-up 3-------------------------

 

{\huge\bf Toss-Up Question}

{\Large Find the area of the ellipse given by the equation $2x^2+y^2=10$.}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $5\sqrt{2}\pi$.}

\vspace{2in}

{\huge\bf Follow-Up Question}

{\Large Find the equation of the ellipse with foci at $(1, 3)$ and $(9,3)$ and a minor axis of length $8$.}

\medskip



\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $\frac{(x-5)^2}{32}+\frac{(y-3)^2}{16}=1$.}

\clearpage

\vspace{0.75in}



%-------------------Question/Follow-up 4-------------------------


{\huge\bf Toss-Up Question}




{\Large A boat follows a hyperbolic path with Island A and Island B as foci. At 10:00am the boat is $10$ miles from Island A and $15$ miles from Island B. At 2:00pm the boat is $8$ miles away from Island A. What are all possible distances between the boat and Island B at 2:00pm?}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $13$ miles or $3$ miles.}

\vspace{2in}

{\huge\bf Follow-Up Question}

{\Large Find the standard form of a hyperbola symmetric about the $y$-axis with one vertex at $(45,0)$ and one focus at $(\sqrt{2061}, 0)$.}



\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $\frac{x^2}{2025}-\frac{y^2}{36}=1$.}\\
