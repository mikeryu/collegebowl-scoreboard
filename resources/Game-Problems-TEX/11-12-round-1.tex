{\huge\bf Toss-Up Question}


{\Large Write the expression $4\ln 3+2 \ln 5$ as a single natural logarithm without exponents.}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $\ln 2025$.}

\vspace{2in}

{\huge\bf Follow-Up Question}

{\Large Simplify completely:
    $$
    \frac{1}{\log_{32} 10}+\frac{5}{\log_5 10}.
    $$}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $5$.}

{\huge\bf Toss-Up Question}




{\Large Over a period of five years, the population of moose in a certain state increased by a factor of five. What was the population's constant annual rate of increase over this period?}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $\sqrt[5]{5}-1\%$.}\ \\

\textbf{Note to Emcee}: After reading the answer for the toss-up question, also read ``This is about $38\%$.''\ \\

\vspace{2in}

{\huge\bf Follow-Up Question}

{\Large The value of a certain car depreciates at a rate of 3\% each year. How long will it take for the car to lose half of its original value?}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $-\frac{\ln 2}{\ln.97}$ years OR $\frac{\ln .5}{\ln.97}$ years
.}\ \\

\textbf{Note to Emcee}: After reading the answer for the follow-up question also read ``This is almost 23 years.''

{\huge\bf Toss-Up Question}


{\Large Suppose $a$, $b$, and $c$ are positive integers such that $a<b<c$ and
    $$
    \log a + \log b + \log c = 1.
    $$
    Find $a$, $b$, and $c$.}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $a=1, b=2, c=5$.}

\vspace{2in}

{\huge\bf Follow-Up Question}

{\Large Find all solutions to $$1+\log_2(x^2-45)=3+\log_2 x.$$}

\medskip



\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $x=9$.}

{\huge\bf Toss-Up Question}


{\Large Let $f(x)=e^{2x}$ and $g(x)=a\ln x$. Find $a$ such that $g(f(x))=x$ for all $x$.}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $a=\frac{1}{2}$.}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}
