{\huge\bf Toss-Up Question}



{\Large Find the third derivative of $e^{2x}$.}





\vspace{0.3in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $8e^{2x}$.}

\vspace{1.5in}

{\huge\bf Follow-Up Question}

{\Large Let $f(x)=xe^{x^2}$. Find the slope of the line tangent to $f'(x)$ at $x=1$. (You may leave your answer in terms of $e$.)}\\


\vspace{0.3in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $10e$.}

\clearpage

%-------------------Question/Follow-up 2-------------------------



{\huge\bf Toss-Up Question}




{\Large Two cars start at the same point. The first car drives North at a speed of $10$ miles per hour and the second drives East at a speed of $20$ miles per hour. What is the distance between the two cars after three hours?}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $30\sqrt{5}$ miles.}

\vspace{2in}

{\huge\bf Follow-Up Question}

{\Large Two cars start at the same point. The first car drives North at a speed of $10$ miles per hour and the second drives East at a speed of $20$ miles per hour. At what rate is the distance between the two cars changing?}

\vspace{0.5in}

{\huge\bf Answer}

{\Large $10\sqrt{5}$ miles per hour.}

\clearpage
